# VeloClear Product Vision

## Why VeloClear Exists

Delivery organisations already have tools: Jira, Azure DevOps, Microsoft Project, Excel, Smartsheet, Power BI, Confluence and PMO templates.

The problem is not lack of tools.

The problem is that the tools do not understand delivery behaviour.

Data is fragmented across:
- task boards
- RAID logs
- project plans
- governance packs
- sprint reports
- steering decks
- spreadsheets
- retrospective notes

VeloClear sits above these tools as the intelligence layer.

## Target Users

### Beginner / Freelancer
Needs help turning a business idea or client request into a structured delivery plan.

VeloClear helps by guiding:
- discovery
- scope definition
- backlog creation
- sprint setup
- risk identification
- stakeholder planning
- governance actions

### Project Manager
Needs lifecycle control, RAID visibility, delivery health and reporting.

VeloClear helps by connecting:
- charter
- WBS
- backlog
- sprint progress
- risks
- issues
- escalations
- reports

### Scrum Master
Needs sprint health, blockers, retrospective learning and delivery flow.

VeloClear helps by tracking:
- velocity
- burn-down
- blocked PBIs
- recurring blockers
- retrospective actions
- future recurrence

### Product Owner
Needs backlog quality, prioritisation, feature traceability and value delivery.

VeloClear helps by connecting:
- business goals
- epics
- features
- PBIs
- sprint outcomes
- acceptance criteria
- delivery risk

### Portfolio Manager / PMO
Needs cross-project risk, systemic drift, governance pressure and delivery confidence.

VeloClear helps by surfacing:
- dependency concentration
- high-risk clusters
- repeated delay causes
- governance bottlenecks
- learning reuse

### Sponsor / Executive
Needs decision clarity.

VeloClear answers:
- Are we on track?
- Why are we not on track?
- What decision is needed?
- What happens if no action is taken?

## Product Promise

VeloClear turns project delivery from manual status reporting into connected delivery intelligence.
