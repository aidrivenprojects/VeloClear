Your view

Three things.
That is all you need to do.

The Team Member view shows you exactly three things. Move your cards. Log blockers. Add retro items. Everything else — risks, governance, EVM, stakeholders — is handled by your PM and completely invisible to you.

🏃
1. Move your cards on the sprint board
Four columns — To Do, In Progress, In Review, Done. Drag your card to the right column. When it lands in Done, the burn-down chart updates automatically and your PM gets a notification. That is your only required action.
🚧
2. Log a blocker when something stops you
If something is stopping your card moving, tap "Log blocker" directly on the card. Your PM is notified immediately. The blocker is tracked. You do not need to send an email, raise a ticket, or attend a meeting. The system handles the escalation.
🔄
3. Add your retrospective items
At the end of each sprint, the retro board opens. Add one card to "What went well" and one to "What to improve." Vote on the items that matter most. The top actions get assigned and tracked automatically.
